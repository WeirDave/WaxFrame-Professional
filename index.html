AI Hive

Orchestrate the Hive

v2.0

AI Hive puts multiple AI assistants to work on your document at the same time. One acts as the Builder — writing and refining. The rest act as Reviewers — giving specific, numbered suggestions. Round by round, your document gets better.

🆓
Free Version
Manual copy/paste workflow. No API keys needed — free tier AIs work fine. You copy prompts to each AI tab yourself and paste responses back. Mix paid and free AIs however you like. If an AI hits its limit, skip it that round.
→
⚡
Pro Version API
Fully automated — one button does everything. Each AI you want to use needs its own API key and paid subscription. Start with just 2 if you like, or use all 6. Your Builder AI processes the full document every round, so it needs a subscription with enough capacity.
→

Who is your Builder?

The Builder receives the full document every round and writes the updated version. Choose carefully — in Pro/API mode your Builder must have a paid API subscription since it processes a lot of tokens every round.
💡 Tip: Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini all handle large documents well and make excellent Builders. Avoid an AI with strict free-tier token limits as your Builder — it will run out quickly and you may not be able to finish your project without an extended wait. Learn more about tokens →
⚙️ You can change your Builder any time from the main screen
