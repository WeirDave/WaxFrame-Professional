────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PROJECT GOAL:
Refine this blog post about home coffee brewing for
publication on a lifestyle and food website targeting
enthusiastic home cooks aged 30–55. Improve the headline,
strengthen the opening hook, and ensure each section flows
into the next. Tone: warm, knowledgeable, not snobby.
Target length: 600–800 words. Optimise for search around
"how to make better coffee at home."

DOCUMENT:
HOW TO MAKE BETTER COFFEE AT HOME

A lot of people don't know that you can make really good
coffee at home without spending a lot of money. In this
article I will share some tips.

THE BEANS MATTER
First of all you should buy good beans. Fresh roasted beans
are better than old ones. Try to buy from a local roaster
if you can.

GRIND YOUR OWN
If you grind your own beans the coffee will taste better.
A burr grinder is better than a blade grinder. They cost
more but it's worth it.

WATER QUALITY
The water you use matters too. If your tap water tastes
bad your coffee will taste bad. Use filtered water.

TEMPERATURE
The water shouldn't be boiling. Around 200 degrees
Fahrenheit is right. Let it sit for 30 seconds after
boiling.

RATIO
Use about 1 to 2 tablespoons of coffee per 6 ounces of
water. Adjust to your taste.

CONCLUSION
If you follow these tips your coffee will be much better.
It takes a little practice but it's worth it.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PROJECT GOAL:
Refine this recipe for a classic chocolate chip cookie.
Improve the clarity of the instructions, check that the
steps are in a logical order, and ensure ingredient
quantities are mentioned at first use. Tone: warm and
beginner-friendly. Add helpful tips where appropriate —

DOCUMENT:
CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

Ingredients
2 and 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 sticks butter, softened
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions
Mix the dry stuff together. Cream the butter and sugars.
Add the eggs and vanilla to the butter mixture. Combine
wet and dry. Stir in the chips. Drop onto baking sheet.
Bake at 375 for about 10 minutes or until done. Cool
before eating.
