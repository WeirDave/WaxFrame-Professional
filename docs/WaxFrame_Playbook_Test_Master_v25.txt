Scope & constraints:     Preserve the factual content of the source review exactly. Vary the voice, length, and structure per platform. Output should be 3 clearly-labeled sections: "Google version", "Tripadvisor version", "Reddit r/[city] version" (or whatever 3 platforms apply).

Length:                  No limit  (each platform version self-bounds)
