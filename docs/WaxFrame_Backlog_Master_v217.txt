Observations

- 2026-06-04 — David's mascot-placement instinct was correct and worth the re-do. The first v3.63.147 cut put the Builder Bee inside every row, which read as repetitive clutter (10 identical mascots stacked vertically when all rows are expanded). One mascot in the header reads as brand presence; ten mascots in the rows reads as design noise. The lesson: brand assets belong in identity-level locations (headers, hero sections, navigation), not in row-level affordances.
