- SHIPPED — Builder-capability filter at recommendation/selection time + clearer truncation error (v3.63.132, this release). Closes the v200 "Builder-capability filter" Open Features entry. The previous entry called for "a `builderCapable: boolean` flag on the model metadata, populated from a known-bad list initially (start: all jamba-*), augmented over time by capture from ring-buffer evidence."
